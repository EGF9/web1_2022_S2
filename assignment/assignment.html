Mānawatia a Matariki

Celebrate Matariki

Matariki rises in late July 2022 marking the beginning of the Māori New Year. To celebrate, we invite you to

organise a Matariki moment.

Invite your whānau and friends to share kai and learn a kaikōhau (expression of hope) in the language of

Aotearoa as well as the languages of your ancestors.
